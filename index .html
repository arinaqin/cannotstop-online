How to Play Monster Survivors

Getting started with Monster Survivors is easy! Use your mouse or touch controls to move your character around the battlefield. Your character will automatically attack nearby monsters, so focus on positioning and dodging enemy attacks.

Collect experience points from defeated monsters to level up and choose from powerful upgrades. Pick up coins and items scattered across the battlefield to enhance your abilities. The key to survival is finding the right balance between offense and defense.

As you progress, you'll encounter boss monsters and special events that provide greater challenges and rewards. Master the art of kiting enemies, managing your resources, and timing your special abilities to achieve the highest survival times.
